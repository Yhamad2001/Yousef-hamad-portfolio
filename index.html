Customer Churn & Revenue Risk Analysis

A complete end-to-end churn analytics pipeline covering 20 accounts and $46,705 in active MRR. SQL models risk from behavioral data, Python assigns individual churn probability scores via logistic regression, Tableau visualizes the risk landscape, and Excel gives leadership a live scenario model showing the dollar impact of reducing churn.

  • Designed a 4-table MySQL schema and authored 15+ SQL queries to calculate churn rates by segment, build a composite health score, and classify all accounts as CRITICAL, HIGH, MEDIUM, or LOW risk
  • Trained a logistic regression model in Python using 7 behavioral features — achieving clean separation between churned accounts (avg probability 0.945) and active accounts (avg 0.028)
  • Developed a 4-sheet Tableau dashboard with a revenue heatmap, customer health scatter plot sized by MRR, and usage trend line — revealing churned customers averaged 6x lower engagement
  • Built an Excel executive summary with a live what-if model: reducing churn from 40% to 25% recovers $93,410 in annual revenue
